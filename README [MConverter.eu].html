Jillian Clasky

Jillian Clasky is a poet and and fiction writer from Toronto. Her work has appeared or is forthcoming in journals such as PRISM international, Room, and Maisonneuve, and she was shortlisted for the 2023 CBC Poetry Prize. She currently lives in Ottawa and serves as editor-in-chief of Common House Magazine.
